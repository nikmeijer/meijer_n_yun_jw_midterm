Debate

New Cars are better

  • Exterior

    • Modern cars have beautiful exterier. Its outline is stunning. Because the modern cars follow the latest car design trends, they fit for the taste of modern people well. A more aerodynamic shape compared to older cars increases fuel efficiency.

  • Interier

    • Modern cars' interior is future-oriented. The active use of lighting satisfies the aesthetic needs of the person in the car. The dashboard actively uses the latest digital technology to increase user convenience, bringing greater satifaction to the driver than in old cars. In particular, HUD and built-in navigation are functions that cannot be found in old cars.

  • Performance

    • With the advancement of automobile manufacturing technology, especially modern cars' engine performance has improved remarkably. As technologies for all mechanical parts such as suspension and brakes have been developed as well as engine performance, not only driving convenience but also driver and passenger safety have been improved significantly.

  • Reliability

    • Since the car is brand new, there are few breakdowns. It is also less expensive to maintain, as automakers offer long warranty periods and warranty services.

  • Economy

    • Although the price for first purchase of a new car is more expensive than an older car, the overall cost is much lower than owning an older car because the cost of maintaining the car is lower. Especially in the case of old cars, it is often difficult to find parts, so it may cost a lot to find them and replace it. Sometimes finding parts can take days and moths. So it costs a lot for the owner to rent other cars to use instead of his old car.
